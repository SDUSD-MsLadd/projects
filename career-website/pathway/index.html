College and Career Pathways

The state of California in coordination with Federal standards identified these career and college pathways to help focus and harness the interests of students into their future adult life. They are:

  1. Agriculture and Natural Resources
  2. Arts, Media and Entertainment
  3. Building and Construction Trades
  4. Business and Finance
  5. Education, Child Development and Family Services
  6. Energy, Environment & Utilities
  7. Engineering and Archetecture
  8. Fashion and Interior Design
  9. Health Science and Medical Technology
  10. Hospitality, Tourism and Recreation
  11. Information and Communication Technologies
  12. Manufacturing and Product Development
  13. Marketing, Sales and Service
  14. Public Services
  15. Transportation

Not all careers/choices fit into a pathway

For this reason we created a separate section to include other professions such as professional athelete and military careers. You can find them here.
