Engineering and Architecture

In California, the Engineering and Architecture pathway is modeled after parts of two national career clusters. Courses in this pathway fall under four areas:

  1. Architectural Design
  2. Engineering Technology
  3. Engineering Design
  4. Environmental Engineering

As with the Energy, Environment, & Utilities cluster, this pathway falls into the ITE program.
