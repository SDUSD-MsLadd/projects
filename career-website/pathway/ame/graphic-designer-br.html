Grapic Desginer

by Braylin Ramos

Job Description

Graphic designers are visual communicators who design many products. Their ideas turn into logos, ads, websites, and product packages. Graphic designers rely on a range of computer programs. A whole project can go for weeks or over a year. Graphic designers are experts on visual communication they use colors, photos, font style and other graphics to convey a message

Education and Training

To become a graphic designer you need to have a high school diploma. You should also probably take art, design, and computer courses in high school. One option is to get a bachelor degree from a 4 year college or an art design school. A second option is to get an associate degree from a related subject from a 2 year college.

Earnings

Junior graphic designers earn about $30,000-$45,000 you're required to have a bachelors' degree in graphic design or related area. Intermediate graphic designers earn about $40,000-$65,000 you're required to have several years of experience as a junior graphic designer. Senior graphic designer / art director earn $50,000- $85,000 you're required to have many years of experience as an intermediate graphic designer, good conceptual thinking abilities, and business or management training. Creative directors earn about $70,000-$120,000 you're required to have many years of experience as a graphic designer and art director and have excellent people skills and experience dealing with clients.

Core Tasks

  • Consult with clients about projects, audiences, and budgets
  • Prepare rough drafts of desgins and present them to clients for approval
  • Gather or create the elements, such as photographs, text, and illustrations
  • Use the collected elements to create a final product, like a poster or website

Workplace Conditions

  • Work for ad agencies, desgin firms, or large companies, or work freelance
  • Work indoors in offices or from home
  • 40 hours a week with some overtime during busy periods
  • Travel reguarly to visit clients
  • Often work alone, and may talk to clients

Hiring Opportinities

Some local companies that may hire people in this career are UC San Diego, Freelancer, Apple, Siege Media, CBRE, Self Employed.com, IBM, and Target.

Sample Career Paths

Title: Entry level

Example tasks:

  1. Handle simple desgins
  2. Assist senior-level desginers
  3. Perfom administrative duties
Title: Mid Level

Example tasks:

  1. Handle big projects from conception to finished product
  2. Deal with clients
  3. Direct junior designers
Title: Senior Level / peak of career

Example tasks:

  1. Develop design concepts and campaigns
  2. Manage designers and other staff
  3. Perform administrative duties such as budgeting

Future Growth and Demands

The graphic desgin field is expected to grow by 3% through 2031.
