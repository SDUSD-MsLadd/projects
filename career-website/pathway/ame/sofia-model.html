Job Description

Models help promote products and services in a wide range of places. Companies hire models to advertise their products on TV, magazines, and conventions. There are many different type of models, some even work as posers for artists and sculptures. Models don't have to be a specific requirement unless asked for, they're all different and unique. Modeling takes a lot of confidence and understanding, each person has their own charm that models use to model. Some models just simply dress up and elegantly walk down a runaway, it involves a lot of strength and confidence. Modeling also requires a lot of recognition. After being asked around to be used as a model, you gain recognition, therefore that helps you gain so much more experience.

Education and Training

Modeling doesn't require any special training or education, the training you could do would be modeling runaway classes in order to look fantastic and to learn how to properly work as a model. Depending on your model work, it's important to learn more than one language since models often travel around and end up in places.

Earnings

  • Models Payment depends more on the job, agency, and situation.
  • Most models earn up to 18k and 49k each year.
  • You could be getting a payment every time you work as a model attend a photoshoot, a tv show, anything, your payments depends on the situation.
  • If you work in an agency your payment is most likely to an amount of money from time to time, could be every month, every week, or so.

    Work and Conditions

    • Models working time also depend on the job and situation, a model could have a photoshoot from 3pm - 7pm, (time varies depending on the situation)
    • Models who are in an agency do indeed have a work time, could be any time of the day.
    • Most models even decide their working time.
    • The work of a model is fun, at the same time there is a lot of stress and time taking work, that is why models need to be understanding, calm, and patient.
    • The workplace of a model could really be anywhere, photoshoots could go outside, overboard, inside, and so does every other situation as a model.

    • Sample Career Paths

    Modeling student:

    Example tasks:

    1. Win around $0 - $5,000
    2. Enrolled in an agency training program.
    3. Good interpersonal skills.
    4. Learn About aspects of modeling, such as posing,movement, expression,health care,appearance, agency procedures.
    Beginner model:

    Example tasks:

    1. Win around $1,000 - $20,000.
    2. Have a portfolio.
    3. Exceed in modeling skills .
    4. Maintain your appearance.
    5. Gain experienece from attending scenes.
    6. Work at a photo shoot and fashion shows.
    National professional Model:

    Example tasks:

    1. Win around $20,000 - $55,000.
    2. Highschool diploma.
    3. 1-3 years of experience in every type of modeling.
    4. Maintain health, appearance.
    5. update portfolio.
    6. Develop Professional modeling.
    International professional model:

    Example tasks:

    1. Win around 40,000 - 1,000,000.
    2. International demand for your modeling services.
    3. 2-5 years of experience.
    4. Work regularly and travel.
    5. Have a schedule.
    6. Maintain your appearance.

    Future Growth and Demands:

    The demand for this career is declining, it employs a smaller amount of people and its growth rate is slower than the national growth for all careers. Most of its growth is due on promotion and recognition. Your task as a model would be to gain experience starting from being a model student to something higher.
