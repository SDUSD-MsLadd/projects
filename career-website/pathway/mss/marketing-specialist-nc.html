Job Description

A market specialist major role is to brainstorm lots of ideas to make a profit for business. Their main target is to show the audience an existing product that can be re-marketed and have customers consume it through data. Therefore, convincing the customers to buy the product on what the company sells in order to gain attention.

In most aspects, the company relies on the majority of the population if a certain product is useful or unique. Furthermore, marketing specialists promote their products through social media, ads, or with other cooperating companies in order to make managing decisions based on a product's characteristics.

In addition, they make sure to visualize and imagine a decent cost on materials and the quality of pricing to place in retailing stores. In addition, a marketing specialist spends lots of time researching the amount of trends an item is decreasing or increasing in popularity. This is to understand if the people care for the need to buy or skip out on the product, in order to come up with the next big thing.

Sample Career Paths

Title: Entry level

Example tasks:

  1. Work Indoors in Offices
  2. Work for marketing or ad agencies, consulting firms, or government agencies, or work freelance
  3. Apply communication and critical thinking towards assisting managers
  4. Work along with product designers, accountants, and ad account executives
Title: Mid Level

Example tasks:

  1. Travel to meet with clients and conduct business launches
  2. Preform sets of campaigns to target the audience
Title: Senior Level / peak of career

Example tasks:

  1. Ensures strategies between goals and major ideas are met in developing measured marketing data
  2. Preform extensive quality in the marketing research to spread awareness in order to promote new products and existing opportunites
