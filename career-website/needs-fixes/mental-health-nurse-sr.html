Education and Training

In order to be a mental health nurse you need: Attend an accredited nursing school. Take the nclex-rn exam and pass it. Earn a psychiatric mental health nurse practitioner degree in order to be able to work as a mental health nurse. Complete the required number of clinical experience hours and become a board-certified psychiatric nurse practitioner
